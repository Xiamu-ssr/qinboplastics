About Qinbo

A professional trading service provider focused on plastic materials

Company Profile

A specialist trader in plastic raw materials

Qinbo deals in commodity, engineering, modified and specialty plastics, thermoplastic elastomers and recycled resins, with particular strength in engineering plastics. We hold import/export rights covering general, entrepot and export trade.

We are a direct distributor for Sinopec, PetroChina, CNOOC, Covestro, Chimei, Formosa, LCY, Nan Ya, SK, LG, BASF, Dow, DuPont, Asahi Kasei, Toray, Mitsubishi and more, shipping most resins directly from multiple Sinopec and Sinotrans warehouses in East China.

With a consultative-selling approach, we analyze the market together with our clients, keep broadening our product range, and provide one-stop sourcing and a stable, reliable materials supply chain.
